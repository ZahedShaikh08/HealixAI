How HealixAI Works

A transparent, safety-first AI system designed to assist — not replace — clinical reasoning.

Machine Learning Engine

HealixAI uses a supervised machine-learning model trained on structured symptom–condition data. User inputs are converted into symptom vectors and evaluated probabilistically.

Medical Safety Layer

Before any output is shown, HealixAI applies rule-based medical safety checks to prevent unsafe or misleading results, especially when symptoms are limited or non-specific.

Confidence & Severity Logic

Confidence levels are dynamically adjusted based on symptom specificity and count. Severity indicators help users understand urgency without claiming diagnosis certainty.

AI Assistant Interface

A conversational AI explains results in plain language, answers health-related questions, and always encourages professional medical consultation.

Ethics, Privacy & Responsibility

HealixAI does not store personal health data permanently, does not automate treatment decisions, and is built strictly as an AI-assisted health awareness system — not a medical authority.
